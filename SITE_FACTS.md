## FAQ Content

**Q: What are the benefits of window replacement for my home?**
Window replacement improves energy efficiency, reduces noise, and enhances security. Upgrading to energy-efficient windows can lower utility bills by keeping indoor temperatures stable. Replacement windows also enhance curb appeal and increase property value.

**Q: How do I know if I need window replacement?**
Common signs include drafts around the frame, condensation or fogging between panes, difficulty opening or closing, rising energy bills, or visible damage to the frame or glass. If your windows are more than 15 to 20 years old, a replacement evaluation is a smart investment.

**Q: What types of replacement windows are available?**
We offer double-hung, casement, picture, sliding, awning, bay, and specialty windows in vinyl, wood, aluminum clad, aluminum thermal break, and fiberglass composite. Every window is custom measured to your home for a precise fit.

**Q: How long does window installation take?**
Most residential projects are completed in a single day. Our crew arrives on time, completes the installation efficiently, and leaves the job site clean before they go.

**Q: What is the difference between single-pane and double-pane windows?**
Single-pane windows have one layer of glass and provide minimal insulation. Double-pane windows have two layers with an insulating gas layer between them, significantly reducing heat transfer, lowering energy costs, and reducing outside noise.

**Q: How much does window replacement cost?**
Cost varies depending on the number of windows, window style, material, and any custom sizing. We offer competitive pricing and financing options. Contact us for a free, no-pressure quote specific to your home.

**Q: Do you offer financing?**
Yes. We offer financing options with fast, one-click approvals to make your window or door replacement affordable. Ask us about current financing offers when you request your free quote.

**Q: What window brands and manufacturers do you work with?**
We work with top-quality manufacturers including Alside and Norandex for windows, and Therma-Tru, Neumadoors, and Masonite for doors. We can also source most major brands on request.
